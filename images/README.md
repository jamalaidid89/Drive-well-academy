# Hero Slideshow Images

Place your professional photos in this folder with the following names:

## Required Images:

1. **automatic-car.jpg** - Your automatic training vehicle
   - High quality, professional photo
   - Shows the car clearly
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px or larger

2. **manual-car.jpg** - Your manual training vehicle (if you offer manual)
   - High quality, professional photo
   - Shows the car clearly
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px or larger

3. **instructor-photo.jpg** - You standing next to the car
   - Professional photo of instructor
   - Shows trust and professionalism
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px or larger

4. **student-l-plates.jpg** - Student holding L-plates
   - Happy student with L-plates
   - Shows success and confidence
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px or larger

5. **driving-lessons-bristol-graphic.jpg** - "Driving Lessons Bristol" graphic
   - Professional graphic/banner
   - Can include your branding
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px or larger

6. **dvsa-training.jpg** - DVSA mock test environment
   - Shows training/test environment
   - Professional setup
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px or larger

## Image Guidelines:

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9 recommended
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (under 500KB per image)
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional photos
- **Content**: Clear, well-lit, professional appearance

## Benefits:

✔️ Builds trust in 2 seconds
✔️ Shows your car, instructor, happy students, L plates
✔️ Helps convert visitors into paying students
✔️ Works perfectly with Google Ads traffic
