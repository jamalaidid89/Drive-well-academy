Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning to drive with us

How many lessons will I need before I can take my test?

Most learners need between 40-50 hours of professional instruction, though this varies based on individual learning pace, practice outside lessons, and natural ability. We'll assess your progress regularly and advise when you're test-ready.

What's the difference between manual and automatic lessons?

Manual lessons teach you to drive with a gear stick and clutch, giving you a full UK driving license valid for both manual and automatic vehicles. Automatic lessons are easier and faster to learn, but your license will only allow you to drive automatic vehicles. We offer both options to suit your needs.

Do you provide the car for the driving test?

Yes! We provide our fully insured, DVSA-approved vehicle for your test. This ensures you're comfortable and familiar with the car, which can significantly improve your chances of passing. The test car is included in our intensive courses.

Can I be picked up from my home or work?

Absolutely! We offer flexible pickup locations throughout Bristol (BS1-BS16). You can be collected from your home, workplace, school, or any convenient location. Just let us know your preferred pickup point when booking.

What happens if I fail my test?

Don't worry - many learners don't pass first time. We'll review your test report together, identify areas for improvement, and create a focused lesson plan to address any weaknesses. We offer refresher lessons and mock tests to build your confidence for the next attempt.

Are your instructors DVSA approved?

Yes, all our instructors are fully qualified ADI (Approved Driving Instructors) registered with the DVSA. Our lead instructor has over 15 years of experience and advanced certifications in road safety engineering.

How do I book lessons?

You can book lessons by calling us on 07514 377171, messaging us on WhatsApp, filling out the contact form on this website, or emailing info@drive-well-academy.com. We'll confirm your booking within 24 hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 48 hours notice for cancellations. Lessons cancelled with less than 48 hours notice may be charged at full rate. We understand emergencies happen - just contact us as soon as possible to rearrange.

Yes! Our AI-powered WhatsApp theory test bot is completely free for all our students. It provides 24/7 support in English and Somali, with practice questions and instant answers to help you prepare for your theory test anytime, anywhere.
